S'mores

Description

What's the most fitting fall/winter treat? The s'more is the most fitting sweet treat in all of it's gooey goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 graham crackers
  • 2 large marshmallows
  • 1 hershey chocolate bar

Steps

  1. Break Crackers in half, as well as the chocolate placing that half chocolate pieces one 2 halves of the graham crackers.
  2. Top each with marshmallows. Placing on a paper towel.
  3. Microwave 10 seconds at a time until the marshmallows start to swell.
  4. Place the top cracker over the marshmallow.
  5. Let stand for 1 minute to soften chocolate.
