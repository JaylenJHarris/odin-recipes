Fruit leather

Description

Does your child's health mean a lot to you? Why not give them a healthy treat that will leave their taste buds happy.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 pounds fresh fruit
  • 1-2 tablespoons Lemon juice
  • 3/4 Sugar(Optional)
  • Spices(Optional)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 200F.
  2. Puree fruit and suger then add lemon juice to taste.
  3. Transfer puree to a saucepan on medium-high heat until it starts to simmer. Them turn the heat to a medium low heat until the mixture is very thick.
  4. Line a 12" by 17" baking sheet with a silicone mat or nonstick foil, using a spatula to spread a thin layer
  5. Bake for 3 hours - 3 hours and 30 minutes
  6. Let is cool completely and then remove the mat/foil. Cut to shape and enjoy.
